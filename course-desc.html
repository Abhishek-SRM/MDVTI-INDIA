Post Graduate Diploma in Yoga Teacher

Course Overview

PG Diploma in Yoga is a 1 year course that can be studied as a skill enhancement course for the people who want to work as a Yoga Teacher. PGDYT course is structured to help individuals become well rounded, competent, professional and highly skilled yoga teachers.

PG Diploma in Yoga eligibility is that the candidates should have a minimum aggregate score of 50%-60% in graduation from a recognized Board with a relevant subject. There are no entrance tests for admission but some may ask for a personal interview or group discussion for final selection in this course.
