What is Project Avahan & Why you should Join this Project?

The aim is to establish influential centers that nurture and shape masters of yoga.’ Project Aavahan represents Yogkulam's earnest endeavor to unite a community of enthusiastic individuals from India and across the globe. This diverse group includes proponents of ancient yogic wisdom and practices, those committed to dedicating their time and energy to cultivate yoga masters worldwide, and individuals possessing a keen business acumen, recognizing the intrinsic value of fitness and the potential for financial gain. In the contemporary landscape, the significance of health and wellness has taken center stage, particularly in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic. Many individuals have embraced yoga as their preferred way of life, drawn by its myriad advantages encompassing enhanced physical and mental well-being, stress reduction, and the fostering of a positive mindset. Consequently, there has been a substantial surge in demand for trained yoga professionals, as people seek guidance on their spiritual journeys. This upswing mirrors the shifting employment market, evolving lifestyle preferences, and a burgeoning yearning for inner tranquility. At Yogkulam, our vision is clear: to impact lives worldwide through the timeless wisdom and traditions of yoga. Project Aavahan serves as a pivotal component of this mission, aimed at establishing influential centers dedicated to nurturing and molding yoga masters.
