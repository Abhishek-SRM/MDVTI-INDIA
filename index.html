What is Yoga Teacher Training?

MDVTI INDIA stands as a premier institution in the realm of Yoga Teacher Training, holding the esteemed position as the leading institute for over 9 years. Our enduring commitment lies in nurturing and fostering well-rounded, proficient, and adept yoga teachers. We achieve this through meticulously crafted and comprehensive courses designed to hone the skills and expertise of aspiring teachers.
Our flagship Yoga Teacher Training (YTT) Course is meticulously structured to provide an immersive experience in Yogic practices, encompassing an in-depth study of Yogic Kriyas, Asanas, Pranayama, and the intricate methodologies of teaching Yoga. Beyond the fundamentals, our curriculum delves into the exploration of profound concepts and techniques intrinsic to Yoga.

The course encompasses multifaceted elements such as Anatomy, Physiology, Psychology, Meditation Techniques, Naturotherapy, and the fundamental essence of Yogic Philosophy. This holistic approach equips budding yoga teachers with a comprehensive understanding and application of Yoga, empowering them to impart knowledge that transcends mere physical practice.
We prioritize the integration of deeper aspects of Yoga, allowing our students to comprehend its transformative power not only on a physical level but also on mental, emotional, and spiritual planes. Through this amalgamation of diverse facets of Yoga, our graduates are prepared to commence their careers in the World of Yoga as proficient and well-versed instructors.

Our Curriculum Offerings

Yoga Teacher Training (YTT) Course

Our flagship program, the Yoga Teacher Training Course, serves as a comprehensive immersion into the world of yoga instruction. This course is meticulously structured to provide a profound understanding of various aspects of yoga, including Yogic Kriyas, Asanas, Pranayama, and Teaching Methodologies.

Advanced Yoga Teacher Training

For those seeking to deepen their expertise, our Advanced Yoga Teacher Training delves into intricate and specialized areas such as Advanced Asana Practice, Yoga Therapy, Chakra Balancing, Energy Work, and Yoga Nidra.
We offer diverse workshops focusing on niche areas like Prenatal Yoga, Yoga for Stress Management, and Yoga for Athletes. Our courses cater to diverse interests, expertise levels, and schedules, ensuring a transformative learning experience.

Commitment to Unparalleled Yoga Education

At MDVTI INDIA, we are committed to curating an unparalleled and immersive yoga learning experience that distinguishes us in the realm of yoga education. Our pride lies in our exceptional team of highly experienced and devoted yoga instructors, each bringing a rich blend of expertise, passion, and a profound dedication to guiding students on their yoga journey.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum goes beyond the ordinary, presenting a comprehensive and holistic fusion of traditional teachings interwoven with innovative modern approaches. We foster a learning environment that encompasses a wide spectrum of knowledge, from in-depth theoretical studies to hands-on practical sessions. Our educational program delves into the profound depths of yoga philosophy, intricacies of anatomy, various meditation techniques, and specialized disciplines, providing students with a well-rounded understanding and skill set.

Inclusive and Supportive Community

What truly sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to cultivating an inclusive and supportive community. Here, every individual's unique path and progress in yoga are celebrated and nurtured. Through this, we empower our students not only to become proficient instructors but also to embody the core principles of yoga in their daily existence.
